PHASE 4 SPECIFICATION – FULL GYM SAAS
This document defines the complete and detailed requirements for Phase 4 of the Gym SaaS project. Phase 4
transforms the Mini Gym SaaS into a full-scale, production-ready Gym Management Platform.
1. Phase 4 Objective
The objective of Phase 4 is to build a scalable, secure, and automation-driven Gym SaaS that handles daily gym
operations end-to-end, including attendance, payments, trainers, and member self-service.
2. Target Customers
•     Gyms with 200–2,000 members
•     Premium fitness studios
•     Multi-trainer gyms and franchises
3. Core Product Principles
•     Automation over manual work
•     Mobile-first experience
•     Clear ROI for gym owners
•     Modular and role-based system
4. Authentication & Roles
•     Role-based login: Owner, Manager, Trainer, Member
•     OTP-based authentication (email/phone)
•     Multi-branch support (optional)
5. Member Management (Advanced)
•     Full member profiles with history
•     Membership upgrades, freezes, transfers
•     Attendance and payment history tracking
6. Attendance Management
•     QR code-based check-in
•     Manual check-in by trainer/admin
•     Batch-wise and time-based attendance rules
7. Memberships & Subscriptions
•     Recurring membership plans
•     Auto-expiry and grace periods
•     Discounts and proration logic

8. Billing & Payments
•     Online payments (UPI/Card)
•     Cash and manual payment tracking
•     Auto invoice generation with tax compliance
9. Trainer & Payroll Management
•     Trainer profiles and schedules
•     Attendance-based or fixed payroll calculation
•     Monthly payroll reports
10. Class & Batch Scheduling
•     Class creation with capacity limits
•     Member enrollment in classes
•     Trainer assignment and calendars
11. Automated Communication Engine
•     Expiry and payment reminders
•     Class updates and cancellations
•     WhatsApp, SMS, Email support
12. Member Portal / App
•     View membership and attendance
•     Online payments and invoice download
•     Notifications and announcements
13. Analytics & Owner Dashboard
•     Active vs expired members
•     Revenue and retention metrics
•     Trainer performance insights
14. What Must NOT Be Built
•     Diet or workout planning
•     Wearable integrations
•     AI fitness coaching
15. Success Criteria
Phase 4 is successful if gyms use the system daily, churn remains below 3%, and the platform is stable enough
for scale and investment.
